Who we are?

We are a consultancy company established by Carisma Emlak, a real estate company with 23 years of industry experience in Istanbul Ataköy. In our office located at JW Marriott Hotel Istanbul Marmara Sea, we offer personalized and high-level services for our real estate investors both at home and abroad.

Why Us?

1. We have a CRM customer network of 46,500 people.

2. We offered the real estate consultancy service to 157 foreign clients so far.

3. We managed a total portfolio of TL 465 Million for the year 2022.

4. From the day we were founded, we have provided service with a total of 6213 transactions to 12,566 people

5. We mediated 90% of the properties for sale are sold within 75 days.

Services

1. Comparative Market Analysis: More than 10 years of industry experience and with our expert team of 18 people who have their competencies in English, Russian and Arabic we offer personalized analysis by comparing with different ads for your real estate to be marketed.

2. Various Means of Transportation: We provide service with our personal vehicle so our guests can reach more comfortable to our office. Also with our boat located at Ataköy Marina We offer the opportunity to see the projects on the Kennedy street from the sea.

3. Real Estate Information File: In order to be able to provide more detailed information about your properties to potential customers we are preparing Turkish English presentation files. In addition, we create pages that contain specialities of properties with the special relay operation for each apartment.

Procedures

1. Renting Procedures: After completing our procedures such as tenant and guarantor credit rating and pay performance research, obtaining the consent of the tenant and the guarantor spouse, the eviction commitment, creation of the fixture list, your rental transaction is performed.

2. Selling Procedures: Our procedure is carried out in a transparent manner with reaching a bilateral agreement and and with the signing of purchase contracts.
